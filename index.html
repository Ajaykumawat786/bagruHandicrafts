Ajay Kumawat

Founder & CEO

With over 15 years of experience in preserving and promoting traditional Indian handicrafts, Ajay founded Bagru Crafts with a vision to bridge the gap between skilled artisans and modern consumers. His passion for authentic craftsmanship and sustainable practices drives our mission to bring you the finest handcrafted products while supporting local artisan communities across India.

"Every product tells a story of tradition, skill, and dedication. We're not just selling products; we're preserving heritage and empowering artisans."
